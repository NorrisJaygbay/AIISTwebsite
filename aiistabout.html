About AIIST

AIIST: Is Africa International Institute of Science & Technology.

AIIST is an Information Technology Training Institute based in Monrovia, Liberia.

AIIST offers practical trainings in Information Technology at three levels.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to promote success in the workplace and in higher education; and to elevate entrepreneurship as well as cultural, social, and economic development.

Our Vision
